Cole's Concert Web Page

What is this page about?

This page is dedicated to my photos and thoughts of concerts I have been to and concerts I hope to attend. I am hoping to share my thoughts on the music I love. Athens has such a rich and vibrant music scene and that has got to be my favorite part of the town. I will share my past experiences attending concerts with an emphasis on those in Athens. Additionally, I will share a list of concerts I hope to attend in the future.

Live music is one of the greatest things we can experience. A beautifully uniting art, music is a blessing and being able to hear, see, and feel it live in the company of strangers is a special time. I think that it is something that a lot of people miss out on and hope that people would be willing to put themselves out there to experience one of my favorite activities. The bands I have mentioned are only a few of the great bands you can see in and around Athens. I encourage everyone to try and see music they are not familiar with. Thank you for checking out my page and come back for more updates on my favorite bands to see live!
