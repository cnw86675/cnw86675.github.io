What is this page about?

This page is dedicated to my reviews of concerts I have been to and concerts I hope to attend. I am hoping to share my thoughts on the music I love. Athens has such a rich and vibrant music scene and that has got to be my favorite part of the town. I will share my past experiences attending concerts with an emphasis on those in Athens. Additionally, I will share a list of concerts I hope to attend in the future.
